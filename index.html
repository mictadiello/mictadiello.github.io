About

Healthcare AI and informatics leader with pluriennial experience building clinical AI systems and data strategy across academic medical centers, health systems, and startups. I am a former clinician and advisor to C-suite leaders across AI/ML, healthcare operations, and venture.

I have built clinical AI infrastructure for research teams, including an AI agent for clinical content retrieval for nurses.

I have published or contributed to peer-reviewed work in JAMA Network Open, JAMIA, Nursing Outlook, Health Equity, the International Journal of Obstetric Anesthesia, and OHDSI, including work on clinical NLP, stigmatizing language in electronic health records, synthetic data augmentation, ChatGPT for clinical documentation, and expansion of the OMOP Common Data Model for healthcare price transparency.

I hold a Master's degree in Management in Clinical Informatics from Duke University School of Medicine and a Bachelor of Nursing from the University of Verona School of Medicine.
